BreathCheck User Guide

What BreathCheck Is
BreathCheck is a self-help app for anxiety support. It guides you through structured learning, practical coping tools, and progress tracking.

Important Safety Note
BreathCheck is not a substitute for professional mental health care or emergency services.
If you feel unsafe or at risk, contact local emergency services or a trusted person immediately.

Privacy
Your data is stored locally on your device.
BreathCheck is designed to keep your personal entries private.

Main Sections
1) Learn
- Follow the 6-module program in order.
- Module 1 starts unlocked.
- Next module unlocks after completing the previous one.
- You can open module overviews and resume from your saved step.

2) Tools
- BreathCheck: guided paced breathing session.
- Thought Log: capture situation, thought, emotion, and balanced alternative.
- Usage history records sessions and saved logs.

3) Progress
- Shows module completion, weekly activity, streaks, and milestones.
- Milestones update as you complete modules and keep using the app.

4) Support
- Save personal emergency contacts and support resources.
- Add and delete entries anytime.

5) Settings
- Change master password.
- Delete account/data.
- Adjust theme and comfort mode.
- Set reminder preferences.
- Export your data.
- Reset progress when needed.

Learn Program (6 Modules)
- Module 1: Understanding Anxiety
- Module 2: Relaxation
- Module 3: Our Thoughts
- Module 4: Changing Thoughts
- Module 5: Coping with Worry
- Module 6: Lifestyle Factors & Relapse Prevention

How Module 1 Works
Module 1 uses a step-by-step format and automatically saves your answers.
You can leave and return later without losing progress.

Module 1 includes:
- Orientation and goals
- Thoughts/Body/Actions map
- Fight-or-flight body signals
- Worry cycle example
- Concern vs worry quiz
- Anxiety domains map
- Values to goals to tiny habits
- Baseline ratings
- Micro-plan
- Completion summary

Thought Log Workflow
- Fill Situation, Automatic Thought, Emotion Intensity, Alternative Thought, Re-rate.
- Save the entry.
- Feedback appears in the app.
- History lets you review earlier entries.

BreathCheck Tool Workflow
- Choose pace and duration.
- Start breathing session.
- Pause/stop anytime.
- Session usage is saved in history.

Tips for Best Results
- Keep entries short and honest.
- Use the app consistently (small daily use is better than occasional long use).
- Update your micro-plan weekly.
- Use Support page contacts if you need immediate real-world help.

Troubleshooting
- If a module button is disabled, complete the previous module first.
- If feedback is unavailable, save still succeeds and fallback guidance may appear.
- If the app feels slow, restart and keep only one app window open.

Data Export
You can export app data from Settings for backup or personal records.

Final Note
Progress is not about perfection. BreathCheck is designed for steady, practical improvement over time.
